Privacy Policy for Readerr (RSVP Speed Reader)
Last updated: June 27, 2026
1. Information We Collect
We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers. All reading data (documents, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device only.
2. Advertising
This app displays ads via Google AdMob for free users. AdMob may collect:

Device identifiers (IDFA) if you grant tracking permission via App Tracking Transparency prompt
General location (country/region)
Device information (model, OS version)

If you decline tracking, AdMob will show contextual ads only (no personal data used).

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
3. In-App Purchases
We offer an "Unlimited" subscription ($2.99/month) processed entirely through Apple. We never see or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy
4. Reading Content
Documents you import (PDF, EPUB, web pages, text) are stored locally on your device only. We cannot access your documents.
5. Analytics
We do not use any third-party analytics services.
6. Children's Privacy
This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
8. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: support@myreaderr.com
